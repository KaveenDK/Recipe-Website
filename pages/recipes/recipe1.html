Sri Lankan Chicken Curry

Home


★★★★★ 278 Ratings

Description:

Sri Lankan Chicken Curry is a rich, aromatic, and spicy dish that showcases the island’s love for bold flavors and coconut-based curries. It’s a staple in Sri Lankan households and is often served with rice, roti, or hoppers.

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces, bone-in for more flavor)
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 2-3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1-inch ginger (minced)
  • 2-3 green chilies (sliced, adjust to taste)
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2-3 cardamom pods
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder (adjust to spice preference)
  • 1 tbsp roasted curry powder
  • 1 cup thick coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup water (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Clean and marinate with turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Set aside for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Temper the Spices: Heat coconut oil, add mustard seeds, cinnamon stick, cardamom pods, and curry leaves.
  3. Sauté the Aromatics: Add onions, then garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Cook until golden.
  4. Add the Spices: Mix in marinated chicken and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Simmer with Coconut Milk: Add coconut milk, bring to a boil, then simmer for 20-25 minutes.
  6. Adjust Seasoning: Taste and add more spices or salt if needed.
  7. Serve: Garnish with fresh curry leaves and serve hot with rice, roti, or hoppers.

Tips for Perfect Sri Lankan Chicken Curry:

  • Use bone-in chicken for a richer flavor.
  • Roasted curry powder is essential for authentic taste.
  • Adjust green chilies and chili powder for spice tolerance.

Cultural Significance:

This dish represents Sri Lanka’s culinary heritage, using coconut milk and spices that reflect the island’s tropical environment. It is commonly served at family gatherings and festivals.
